About Us

Created from a small bedroom in Queens, NY. I sell forever friends. With animals who are left alone in various circumstances, my job is to care for them and help them reach a stronger point in their lives and share the love with an owner they can trust. As of now, I only sell various dog and cat breeds however there is possibility in the future for more pet opportunities.There are many who hope to fill some part of themselves with the loyalty that can be found from a pet. Same expected of the poor animals who simply try to find that loving owner. Those who hope to find their future with a loving pet will definitely find their way to us.
